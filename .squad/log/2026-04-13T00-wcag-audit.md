# Session Log — WCAG 2.1 AA Accessibility Audit

**Date:** 2026-04-13
**Requested by:** AJ Enns
**Agents:** Dom (Lead), Mia (Frontend Dev)

AJ requested a WCAG 2.1 AA compliance audit. Dom performed an architectural-level audit (semantic structure, landmarks, ARIA, keyboard, contrast, live regions) finding 22 issues across 10 categories. Mia performed a component-level audit (images, forms, interactive elements, focus, dynamic content) finding 15 issues. After deduplication, the audits identified overlapping concerns on cart link labeling, size selector ARIA states, checkout error announcements, focus indicators, add-to-cart live regions, hero contrast, and footer placeholder links. Combined unique findings merged into decisions.md with a recommended 4-sprint remediation plan. Critical items: skip link, cart accessible name, checkout error role="alert", aria-live regions.
